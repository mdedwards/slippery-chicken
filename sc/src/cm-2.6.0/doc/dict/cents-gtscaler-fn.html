[Function]
(cents->scaler cents)

Convert cents value to a scaler value.

Examples:

Example 1. Converting cents to multiplier on frequency. 

(cents->scaler 100)
⇒ 1.0594630943592953
(note (* 440 (cents->scaler -1100)) :hz #t)
⇒ bf3
