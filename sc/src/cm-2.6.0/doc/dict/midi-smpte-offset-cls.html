[Class]
midi-smpte-offset

A MIDI SMPTE offset event.

midi-smpte-offset supports the following slot initializations:

:time number
The start time of the object.
:offset list
A five-element list defining the SMPTE offset: (hours mins secs frames fractional-frames)

Examples:

Example 1. The midi-smpte-offset event. 

;;; 01:06:12:09:06
(new midi-smpte-offset :time 0 :offset '(1 6 12 9 6))
